About Us

Cozinha de Sonia is a family-run restaurant in Portimão, we serve fresh, homemade Indian vegetarian and vegan food.

All our dishes are prepared daily using authentic Indian recipes, natural spices, and carefully selected ingredients. We believe good food should be both flavourful and nourishing, which is why everything is cooked with care, just like at home.

Our menu offers a variety of traditional vegetarian and vegan dishes, suitable for everyone — whether you follow a plant-based diet or simply enjoy honest, homemade food.
